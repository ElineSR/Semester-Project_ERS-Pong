Velcome to a game of Pong!

How to play

  • Choose a name, difficulty, mode and then press "start game"
  • When the game starts, move your padle up and down to prevent the ball from hitting the wall behind your padle.
  • You get a point everytime the ball is bounced off your padle
  • The game ends when the ball hits the wall behind the padle
